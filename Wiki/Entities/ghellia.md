# Ghellia

## Summary

Ghellia is an Academy teacher who referred Mirian to Lecne. Lecne knows Ghellia and says he taught them soul magic.

## Relevant Details

- Lecne assumes Ghellia sent Mirian to him.
- Lecne is pleased that Ghellia is still teaching at the Academy, though he says he could not tolerate school life himself.
- Mirian says Ghellia likely does not know the details of the Akanan spy situation.
