# Ghellia

## Canon Summary

Ghellia is the person who referred [[mirian|Mirian]] to [[lecne|Lecne]]. Lecne knows them, taught them soul magic, and believes they are still teaching at [[academy|the Academy]].

## Known Facts

- Lecne identifies Ghellia as the likely person who sent Mirian.
- Ghellia is still teaching at the Academy.
- Lecne taught Ghellia soul magic.
- Lecne says he wishes Ghellia had sent him a letter because he loves spy drama.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Teacher at [[academy|the Academy]].
- Former or current student of Lecne in soul magic.
- Connected to Mirian as the referral source.
