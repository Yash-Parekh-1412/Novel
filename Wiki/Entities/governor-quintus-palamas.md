# Governor Quintus Palamas

## Canon Summary

Governor of Palendurio during the events of Chapter 2. Stationed at Ducastil complex with parliamentary offices. His fate after the Akanan Embassy attack (scheduled for 20th of Solem) is unknown - he is never heard from after the attack, suggesting capture, death, or disappearance.

## Known Facts

- Governor of Palendurio
- Member of the Palamas family (same family as King Aurelius)
- Office located at Ducastil complex
- Fate becomes central mystery after embassy attack

## Relationships And Affiliations
