# Palendurio

## Canon Summary

Palendurio is a large city built on a karst landscape with numerous stone spires and limestone formations. The city was sacked and burned twice during the Unification War before being rebuilt with modern designs. Unlike Cairnmouth, Palendurio features broader avenues and is protected by spellwards, allowing it to sprawl beyond its medieval walls. An underground cave-canal system beneath the city was engineered by geomancers to replace dangerous sinkholes, dividing into northern and southern networks bisected by the Magrio River.

## Known Facts

- Built on karst limestone formations creating caves and sinkholes
- Divided by the Magrio River into north and south sections
- Contains an underground canal network maintained by spell engines
- Features both medieval architecture in the city core (protected by Spellwards) and modern buildings outside
- Riverport Station connects the city via rail and river transport
- Grand Bridge crosses the Magrio River at the western end of the city
- Populated by diverse peoples speaking many languages: Baracueli, Eskanar, Adamic, Tlaxa, Gulwenen, Zhighuan
- Known for distinctive but unpleasant smell
- Much larger than Cairnmouth with significant international presence
- Contains multiple embassies including Akana Praediar, Zhighua, and Tlaxhuaco in Tenedor Plaza

## Geography and Districts

- **Kingmont Hill**: Largest limestone formation, site of Charlem Palace
- **Charlem Palace**: Seat of the monarchy atop Kingmont Hill, built with gleaming white limestone and ancient Lorcadian calligraphy
- **Grand Sanctum**: Ancient temple at base of Kingmont Hill facing the river, containing artifacts of the Prophets, decorated with onyx and colorful marble
- **Ducastil**: Limestone formation across river from palace, contains governor's mansion and parliamentary offices
- **Chimera Gate**: Southern entrance into the city core
- **Tenedor Plaza**: Embassy district west of the river, contains embassies of Akana Praediar, Zhighua, and Tlaxhuaco
- **Riverport Station**: Western terminus with Grand Bridge crossing

## Locations of Interest

- **Great Library of Palendurio**: Archive location where Mirian conducts research
- **Bard and the Lion Inn**: Hotel with balcony overlooking Grand Sanctum entrance and embassies
- **Royal Couriers Office**: Records bureau

## Organizations and Authority

- **Palendurio Guard**: City guard that regularly patrols key areas like Ducastil
- **Parliament**: Meets in domed structure by river
- **Governor's Office**: Located at Ducastil

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Historical conflict with Persama during Unification War
