# Academy

## Canon Summary

The Academy is an educational institution connected to [[mirian|Mirian]] and [[ghellia|Ghellia]]. Lecne says he dislikes schools, Academy politics, and dealing with students and parents.

## Known Facts

- Ghellia teaches at the Academy.
- Mirian says everyone at the Academy is a young adult.
- Lecne mentions Academy politics, grades, tests, and parents pulling strings.
- Mirian may still be a student, though she answers "sort of" when Lecne asks.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Workplace of [[ghellia|Ghellia]].
- Connected to Mirian's student status.

## Appearances

- Mentioned in Chapter 1.

## Continuity Questions
