# High Priestess Arenthia

## Canon Summary

High Priestess Arenthia is named by [[lecne|Lecne]] after he accepts [[mirian|Mirian]] as a prophet. The chapter implies she is the colleague whose death Lecne is mourning and the person Mirian intends to save in a future loop.

## Known Facts

- Lecne says the old tradition of kneeling and kissing a prophet's knuckles is something Arenthia would have insisted on.
- When Mirian asks whether Arenthia is the one she is saving, Lecne asks if Mirian can really do it.
- Lecne had earlier said a colleague died the previous day.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Associated with Lecne and the hidden temple of Zomalator.
- Likely associated with [[zomalator|Zomalator]] by title and context.

## Appearances

- Mentioned near the end of Chapter 1.

## Continuity Questions

- The chapter implies, but does not directly state, that Arenthia is Lecne's deceased colleague.
- Her role, powers, and exact rank within the temple are not yet explained.
