# King Aurelius Palamas

## Canon Summary

Current monarch of Baracuel. Resides at Charlem Palace atop Kingmont Hill in Palendurio. His actions and fate during the Akanan Embassy attack and its aftermath are central to Mirian's investigation.

## Known Facts

- King of Baracuel
- Member of the Palamas family
- Resides in Charlem Palace (white limestone buildings with orange/white lion banners)
- Palace decorated with elaborate calligraphy in ancient Lorcadian language
- Fate after embassy attack unknown to Mirian

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Related to Governor Quintus Palamas
- Head of Baracuel monarchy (largely symbolic at this point)
