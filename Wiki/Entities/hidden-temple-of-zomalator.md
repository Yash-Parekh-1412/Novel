# Hidden Temple Of Zomalator

## Canon Summary

The hidden temple of [[zomalator|Zomalator]] is concealed behind a thick patina-covered bronze door inside a brick building reached through winding city alleys. It contains marble floors, murals and paintings of gods, priests in gray vestments, a basement ritual room, and anti-divination wards.

## Known Facts

- Lecne warns Mirian that she is sworn to secrecy about the place.
- The interior resembles a temple more than a dwelling.
- A mural depicts Zomalator as a pale corpse surrounded by blood-vein-like growths.
- A basement room contains incense, a stone sarcophagus, glyph lamps, an altar, and animal siphons used in the soul-mark removal rite.
- Lecne says Mirian may stay there if she helps cook, clean, and check anti-divination wards.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Used by Lecne and other priests or devouts of [[zomalator|Zomalator]].
- Connected to [[high-priestess-arenthia|High Priestess Arenthia]] by context.
- Provides refuge to [[mirian|Mirian]] in Chapter 1.

## Appearances

- Main location of Chapter 1.

## Continuity Questions

- The city containing the temple is not directly named in the immediate temple-arrival scene.
- The full membership and hierarchy of the temple community are not yet known.
