# Torrviol

## Canon Summary

Torrviol is a location tied to Mirian's recent danger and future war events. Mirian says she left Torrviol after stepping into a network of Akanan spies, and she predicts an Akanan invasion heading there.

## Known Facts

- Mirian says one agent may have seen her leave Torrviol.
- Mirian says there is a hive of Akanan spies in Torrviol.
- Mirian mentions the Battle of Torrviol when explaining the time loops.
- Mirian predicts Cairnmouth will learn of the Akanan invasion heading for Torrviol by the 25th of Solem.
