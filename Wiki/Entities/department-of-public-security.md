# Department Of Public Security

## Canon Summary

The Department of Public Security is an organization associated with secret necromancy and soul-marks. [[lecne|Lecne]] calls them "the Deeps" and says they will kill to keep their necromantic practices hidden.

## Known Facts

- Lecne guesses the Deeps when Mirian describes being cursed.
- Mirian asks whether it is not unusual for the Department of Public Security to use necromancy.
- Lecne says their necromancy is a well-kept secret.
- Lecne says people who try to make that information public may be killed.
- Lecne says Mirian is not the first person he has seen cursed by them.
- Mirian believes a double agent connected to the current crisis killed colleagues and a Praetorian.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Secretly connected to necromancy and soul-marks.
- Hidden or uncomfortable relationship with [[luminate-order|the Luminate Order]] by Lecne's account.
- Threatens or pursues [[mirian|Mirian]] by context.
