# Zomalator

## Summary

Zomalator is an underground or little-known god associated with Lecne's hidden temple and with soul-related rites. Lecne calls Zomalator the redeemer and treats service to him as a religious duty.

## Relevant Details

- A mural depicts Zomalator as a pale corpse surrounded by blood-vein-like growths resembling a forest or coral reef.
- A larger painting shows a colossal ivory corpse in a valley, with worshippers around it and another group excavating the body.
- Lecne says Mirian is sworn to secrecy about the temple.
- Lecne says his group teaches people in service of the redeemer, not simply because someone asks for help.
