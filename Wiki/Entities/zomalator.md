# Zomalator

## Canon Summary

Zomalator is an underground or little-known god associated with a concealed temple, soul-related rites, and Lecne's religious duty. [[lecne|Lecne]] calls Zomalator "the redeemer" and says the temple teaches in service of him.

## Known Facts

- Lecne identifies a mural of Zomalator to Mirian and says she probably has not heard of him.
- The mural shows a pale corpse surrounded by blood-vein-like growths that resemble a forest or coral reef.
- A larger painting shows a colossal ivory corpse in a valley, with worshippers around it and another group excavating it.
- Lecne frames helping Mirian as his duty after deciding she is a prophet.
- Lecne says his group teaches people in service of the redeemer, not merely because someone asks for help.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Worshipped or served by Lecne and the priests of [[hidden-temple-of-zomalator|the hidden temple]].
- Connected to [[high-priestess-arenthia|High Priestess Arenthia]] by temple context.
- Associated with soul magic and the soul-mark removal rite by context.

## Appearances

- Mentioned and depicted in Chapter 1.

## Continuity Questions

- The chapter does not fully define Zomalator's domain, doctrine, or relationship to other gods.
- The identity of the differently shaped colossal corpse in the painting is unresolved.
