# Cairnmouth

## Canon Summary

Cairnmouth is the city or area where Chapter 1's hidden temple sequence appears to take place. Lecne says a moon flicker would not have been findable there, and Mirian says Cairnmouth will learn of the Akanan invasion heading for Torrviol by the 25th of Solem.

## Known Facts

- Lecne says he could not have found a moon flicker in Cairnmouth.
- Mirian predicts that Cairnmouth will learn of the Akanan invasion heading for Torrviol.
- The chapter describes narrow, winding streets and tall brick buildings in the part of the city Mirian traverses.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Contains or is closely associated with [[hidden-temple-of-zomalator|the hidden temple of Zomalator]].
- Connected to future war news involving [[torrviol|Torrviol]].

## Appearances

- Mentioned in Chapter 1.

## Continuity Questions

- The chapter does not directly state whether the hidden temple is in Cairnmouth, though the dialogue strongly suggests it.
