# Lecne

## Canon Summary

Lecne is a priest or devout of [[zomalator|Zomalator]] who brings [[mirian|Mirian]] into a concealed temple, helps remove her soul-mark curse, and eventually accepts her as a prophet. He has personal knowledge of soul magic, the Luminate Order, and the Department of Public Security's hidden necromancy.

## Known Facts

- He guides Mirian through narrow city alleys to a hidden temple.
- He knows [[ghellia|Ghellia]] and taught them soul magic.
- He refuses to teach Mirian soul magic at first because she is a stranger and not a devout of Zomalator.
- He says the Department of Public Security's necromancy is a well-kept secret and that public exposure can get someone killed.
- He says he reached the second layer of Luminate Order secrets before leaving the Order.
- He is grieving a colleague who died the previous day.
- He accepts Mirian's account after connecting her shattered-stars dream to a Luminate secret about the Cataclysm.
- He kneels and kisses Mirian's knuckles as an old tradition after deciding she is a prophet.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Devout or priest of [[zomalator|Zomalator]].
- Formerly connected to [[luminate-order|the Luminate Order]].
- Knows [[ghellia|Ghellia]].
- Grieves [[high-priestess-arenthia|High Priestess Arenthia]] or a colleague associated with that title; the chapter strongly implies Arenthia is the person Mirian intends to save.

## Appearances

- Appears throughout Chapter 1 as Mirian's guide, healer, and eventual ally.

## Continuity Questions

- The full reason Lecne left the Luminate Order is not detailed.
- The chapter does not fully confirm Arenthia's exact relationship to Lecne beyond the implication that she is the dead colleague Mirian vows to save.
