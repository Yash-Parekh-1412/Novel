# Lecne

## Summary

Lecne is a priest or devout of Zomalator who shelters Mirian in a hidden temple and helps remove her soul-mark curse. He is grieving a colleague's sudden death but agrees to help Mirian after hearing her time-loop testimony.

## Relevant Details

- Lecne knows Ghellia and taught them soul magic.
- He refuses to teach Mirian soul magic at first because she is a stranger and not a devout of Zomalator.
- He says the Department of Public Security's necromancy is a well-kept secret and that people who expose it may be killed.
- He has personal experience with uncomfortable Luminate Order politics and reached the second layer of Order secrets before leaving.
- After accepting Mirian as a prophet, he kneels and kisses her knuckles as an old tradition.
