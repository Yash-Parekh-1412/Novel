# Luminate Order

## Canon Summary

The Luminate Order is a religious or institutional organization that keeps layered secrets, including a second-layer teaching about shattered stars and the Cataclysm. [[lecne|Lecne]] formerly belonged to the Order and criticizes it as hollowed-out for failing to rally to [[mirian|Mirian]] as a new prophet.

## Known Facts

- Lecne says the Order keeps many secrets and reveals more as members rise in rank.
- Lecne reached the second layer of secrets before he left.
- The second-layer secret includes stars bursting apart in circles of misty flame, the War of the Elder Gods, and prayer to the Guardian.
- Lecne says the Order has unwritten agreements with shadowed parts of the government.
- Lecne says people learn to stay silent about those agreements because saying the wrong thing can lead to expulsion.
- Lecne says members know to turn in anyone with a soul-mark like Mirian had.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Former organization of [[lecne|Lecne]].
- Has uncomfortable hidden ties to shadowed government activity and [[department-of-public-security|the Department of Public Security]] by context.
- Interprets or guards secrets related to gods, prophecy, and the Cataclysm.

## Appearances

- Discussed in Chapter 1.

## Continuity Questions

- The exact nature of its government agreements remains unnamed.
