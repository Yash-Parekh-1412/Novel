# Akana Praediar

## Canon Summary

Akana Praediar is a nation with diplomatic representation in Palendurio. The nation has a Prime Minister and maintains embassies in multiple cities. On the 20th of Solem, the Akanan Embassy in Palendurio is scheduled to be massacred, with retaliatory attacks following on Akanan businesses. The Prime Minister is assassinated following the embassy attack. An Akanan invasion is directed toward Torrviol after these events.

## Known Facts

- Has embassy in Tenedor Plaza in Palendurio
- Has spies operating in Torrviol (mentioned in Chapter 1)
- Led by a Prime Minister
- Embassy is scheduled for attack on 20th of Solem
- Following the massacre, retaliatory attacks occur on Akanan businesses in Palendurio
- Information about the attack spreads rapidly to Akana Praediar
- Prime Minister assassinated after the embassy attack
- Directs military invasion toward Torrviol

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Diplomatic relations with Baracuel (ambassador in Palendurio)
- In conflict with other nations (Persama mentioned)
- Historical conflict with southern powers regarding Alkazaria

## Appearances

- Embassy massacre plotted in Chapter 1 (foreseen by Mirian)
- Primary focus of Chapter 2 investigation
- Referenced in both Chapter 1 and 2
