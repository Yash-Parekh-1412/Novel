# Palendurio Guard

## Canon Summary

The guard force that provides security for Palendurio and its key installations including Ducastil. They patrol regularly and conduct security checks at key locations.

## Known Facts

- Patrols key areas like Ducastil complex regularly
- Posts guards at major staircases and entry points
- Checks anyone attempting to access restricted areas
- Only allows members of parliament to pass some barriers without additional checking

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Reports to Palendurio government/Governor
- Coordinates with parliamentary security

## Appearances

- Active in Chapter 2 surveillance of Ducastil
