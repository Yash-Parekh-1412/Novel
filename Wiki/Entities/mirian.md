# Mirian

## Canon Summary

Mirian is a student connected to the Academy who arrives at [[hidden-temple-of-zomalator|the hidden temple of Zomalator]] while weakened by a soul-mark curse. After the temple rite restores her aura and clarity, she tells [[lecne|Lecne]] about time loops, political disasters, the Battle of Torrviol, other time travelers, and the coming apocalypse.

## Known Facts

- She follows Lecne through winding city alleys to a concealed temple.
- She has black tendrils around her soul before the temple rite breaks them open.
- She identifies a moon flicker as the siphon originally used in the soul-marking.
- She says an agent may have seen her leave [[torrviol|Torrviol]], but would not know her destination.
- She says she encountered Akanan spies in Torrviol and believes a double agent killed colleagues and a Praetorian.
- She claims repeated knowledge of future disasters, including revolt, riots, assassination, invasion, grand auroras, and apocalypse.
- Lecne concludes she is a new prophet after hearing her account and the detail about shattered stars.

## Relationships And Affiliations

- Seeks help from [[lecne|Lecne]] after being referred by [[ghellia|Ghellia]].
- Knows or attends [[academy|the Academy]].
- Opposed or endangered by agents connected to [[department-of-public-security|the Department of Public Security]].
- Becomes linked to [[zomalator|Zomalator]] when Lecne decides helping her is his duty.

## Appearances

- Appears throughout Chapter 1 as the viewpoint character.

## Continuity Questions

- The chapter does not identify the double agent by name.
- The exact mechanism that connects Mirian, the Ominian, and multiple time travelers remains unresolved.
