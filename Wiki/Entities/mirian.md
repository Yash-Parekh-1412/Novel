# Mirian

## Summary

Mirian is a time traveler and Academy-linked student who arrives at Lecne's hidden temple while suffering from a soul-mark curse. She knows future disasters in detail and uses that knowledge to convince Lecne to help her.

## Relevant Details

- Mirian was cursed by a Deeps or Department of Public Security agent who may also be an Akanan double agent.
- She knows Ghellia, who referred her to Lecne.
- After the ritual, Mirian immediately regains clarity and identifies the original siphon as a moon flicker.
- She promises Lecne that his dead colleague can live in a future cycle if he helps her.
- Lecne ultimately treats her as a prophet after hearing her testimony.
