# Chapter 1 Zomalator Temple

## Overview

Chapter 1 follows [[mirian|Mirian]] as [[lecne|Lecne]] brings her to [[hidden-temple-of-zomalator|the hidden temple of Zomalator]], where a rite removes her soul-mark curse. The chapter then pivots from refuge and healing into a larger continuity reveal: Mirian tells Lecne about time loops, future disasters, and the apocalypse, and Lecne recognizes her as a prophet.

## Key Events

- Mirian follows Lecne through disorienting city alleys into a secret temple.
- Mirian collapses and is treated by Lecne and other priests.
- The rite breaks black tendrils around Mirian's soul and restores her aura.
- Lecne identifies Department of Public Security necromancy and the Luminate Order's uncomfortable silence around it.
- Mirian offers to save Lecne's recently deceased colleague and gives a sequence of future events to prove her knowledge.
- Lecne connects Mirian's shattered-stars dream to a Luminate secret about the Cataclysm.
- Lecne accepts Mirian as a prophet and agrees to help her save [[high-priestess-arenthia|High Priestess Arenthia]].
