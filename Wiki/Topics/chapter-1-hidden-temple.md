# Chapter 1 Hidden Temple

## Summary

Mirian follows Lecne through winding city alleys to a concealed temple of Zomalator. After collapsing from the soul-mark curse, she is restored through a ritual involving a marble sarcophagus, incense, priests, and improvised animal siphons.

## Key Events

- Lecne brings Mirian into a hidden temple with marble floors, murals of the Gods, and an underground Zomalator presence.
- Priests remove or break apart the black tendrils around Mirian's soul, restoring her aura and clarity.
- Mirian tells Lecne about the time loops, the Battle of Torrviol, the coming political disasters, and the final apocalypse.
- Lecne connects Mirian's dream of shattering stars to a secret Luminate teaching about the Cataclysm.
- Lecne accepts Mirian as a new prophet and agrees that helping her is his duty.
