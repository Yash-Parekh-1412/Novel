# Chapter 2: Palendurio Embassy

## Overview

Mirian arrives in Palendurio to investigate the planned attack on the Akanan Embassy scheduled for the 20th of Solem. She takes rooms at the Bard and the Lion Inn and spends the chapter conducting reconnaissance on key locations including the embassy, Ducastil (the governor's complex), and parliamentary areas. She develops a new spell called "night camouflage" that combines light absorption and projection for aerial reconnaissance.
