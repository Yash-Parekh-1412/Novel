# Night Camouflage Spell

## Overview

A complex spell invented by Mirian that combines multiple magical techniques to provide aerial invisibility. The spell wraps the caster in shadow and projected light that matches the sky, making the user nearly invisible when airborne.

## Spell Mechanics

**Components:**
- _absorb light_ - Creates veil of shadow around the caster
- _light detection map_ - Detects light patterns from above
- _project light map_ - Re-projects detected light patterns
- _major disguise_ - Coordinate systems incorporated
- Enhancement runes broadening absorption profile

**Technique:**
The spell combines these glyphs with precise sequencing, changing binding points, altering mental components, and adding linking glyphs to prevent flux changes. The resulting spell is significantly complex and requires precise scribing.

## Functionality

- Creates shadow veil around caster
- Detects astronomical light patterns and re-projects them within the veil
- Provides effective camouflage in open sky
- Defeats infrared hunting spells through broadened light absorption
- Does not provide defense against physical attacks

## Development Notes

- Developed by Mirian for aerial reconnaissance
- Scripted on a single page using Mirian's precise techniques
- Tested above Palendurio successfully

## Applications

- Aerial reconnaissance
- Scouting fortified positions
- Infiltration operations

## Limitations

- Requires levitation or flight to be effective
- Levitation still requires significant mana expenditure
- Does not provide invisibility at ground level
- Vulnerable to direct physical detection
