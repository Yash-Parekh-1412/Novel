# Luminate Order Hidden History

## Summary

Lecne reveals that the Luminate Order keeps layered secrets. At the second layer, he learned a passage about stars bursting apart, the War of the Elder Gods reaching Enteria, and the people praying to the Guardian.

## Key Points

- Lecne connects Mirian's dream of shattering stars to the Cataclysm, an event nearly 4000 years old.
- Lecne says the Luminate Order keeps many secrets and that higher ranks learn more of them.
- Lecne describes the Gods as communicating in ways that are not ordinary language and are difficult for humans to understand.
- After hearing Mirian's story, Lecne concludes that she is a new prophet and that the Gods do not speak about insignificant things.
