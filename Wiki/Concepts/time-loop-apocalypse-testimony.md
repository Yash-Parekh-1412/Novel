# Time-Loop Apocalypse Testimony

## Summary

Mirian convinces Lecne by revealing future events, her repeated loops, and the apocalypse she remembers. Her testimony reframes Lecne's grief: the dead can still live in a future cycle if Mirian succeeds.

## Key Points

- Mirian predicts revolt in Persama, danger to Alkazaria, the Akana Praediar embassy massacre, riots in Palendurio, the Akanan Prime Minister's assassination, and the Akanan invasion moving toward Torrviol.
- She also predicts visible magical signs, including a geyser south of the city, failed spellwards, and grand auroras before the end.
- Mirian says she has watched friends die across repeated loops and remains alone with those memories.
- She tells Lecne about the loops, the Battle of Torrviol, other time travelers, Ominian dreams, Rift Sea conspiracies, and the Divir moon crashing down.
