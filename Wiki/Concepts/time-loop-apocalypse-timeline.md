# Time-Loop Apocalypse Timeline

## Summary

Mirian uses future events from repeated loops to persuade Lecne that his colleague can be saved and that the current world-ending sequence is not final. Her account includes political upheaval, assassination, war, magical failure, and the Divir moon crashing down.

## Key Points

- Mirian says a revolt in Persama will begin in six days and threaten Alkazaria.
- She says news will reach Torrviol in eight days and likely reach Cairnmouth earlier.
- She predicts the Akana Praediar embassy massacre in eighteen days.
- She predicts riots in Palendurio and the Akanan Prime minister's assassination.
- She says a magical geyser will erupt south of the city, spellwards will fail, and an Akanan invasion will head for Torrviol.
- She describes grand auroras heralding the end and the Divir moon crashing down.
- She tells Lecne about time loops, the Battle of Torrviol, other time travelers, dreams of the Ominian, and conspiracies across the Rift Sea.
