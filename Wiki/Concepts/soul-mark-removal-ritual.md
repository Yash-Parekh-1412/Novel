# Soul-Mark Removal Ritual

## Summary

The Zomalator priests can treat a soul-mark curse by working directly on the soul. Mirian experiences black tendrils around her soul cracking open into threads of light, then wakes with her aura restored.

## Key Points

- The ritual takes place in a basement room with incense, priests, and a white marble sarcophagus veined with red stone.
- Lecne says the rite went better than expected and that the priests improvised because they did not know what the original caster used as the siphon.
- Mirian identifies the original siphon as a moon flicker.
- Three animal bodies on the altar are withered and blackened after the ritual, implying they served as substitute siphons.
