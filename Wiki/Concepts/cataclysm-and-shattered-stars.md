# Cataclysm And Shattered Stars

## Summary

Lecne connects Mirian's dream of shattered stars to a second-layer Luminate Order secret about the Cataclysm. The secret describes stars bursting apart in circles of misty flame, the War of the Elder Gods coming to Enteria, and people praying to the Guardian for salvation.

## Key Points

- Mirian says the Ominian saw the stars shatter in a dream.
- Lecne recites a secret Luminate passage about stars bursting apart in circles of misty flame.
- Lecne says the passage refers to the Cataclysm.
- Mirian says the Cataclysm was nearly 4000 years ago.
- Lecne argues that gods do not communicate through human language and that Mirian has been chosen as a new prophet.
