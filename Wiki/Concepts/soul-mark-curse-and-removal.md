# Soul-Mark Curse And Removal

## Summary

Mirian arrives at the hidden temple with black tendrils encircling her soul, which Lecne and the temple priests treat as a soul-mark curse. The removal rite restores her aura and clarity, using a sarcophagus, incense, priests, and improvised animal siphons.

## Key Points

- Mirian perceives her own soul and sees threads of light crack open the black tendrils around it.
- She wakes with her aura restored and her brain fog gone.
- Lecne says he did not know what the original siphon was, so the priests improvised.
- Mirian identifies the original siphon as a moon flicker.
- Lecne connects soul-marks to Department of Public Security necromancy.
- Lecne says Luminates know to turn in anyone with a soul-mark like Mirian had.
